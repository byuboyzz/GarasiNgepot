Drift Was Born.

On February 22, 2017, armed with nothing but a camera and a dream, Ziko and Dipo created their first video for the Garasi Drift YouTube channel. Their vision was simple yet profound to focus on the dynamic world of automotive lifestyle Their dedication and talent propelled them to reach 1 million subscribers by 2020, transforming Garasi Drift into a renowned Indonesian automotive phenomenon reaching millions of fans with the epic videos you love.

Garasi Drift has evolved into Indonesia's premier automotive media production, offering a range of high-quality content to its dedicated audience. Specializing in automotive lifestyle videos, the channel covers diverse topics such as car modifications, reviews, event coverage, and their signature drifting videos.
