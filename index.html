GARASI DRIFT

Welcome to Garasi Drift, where passion for automobiles and a knack for storytelling converge. Founded in 2017 by two brothers, Ziko and Dipo, Garasi Drift has grown from a YouTube channel into Indonesia's foremost automotive media production. Our journey started with a shared love for cameras, a passion for drifting, and a desire to capture the essence of the automotive lifestyle.
